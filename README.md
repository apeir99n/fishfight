# Fish Fight

> A 2D browser-based fighting game where fish battle on dry land.

**Genre:** 2D Fighting Game (Mortal Kombat / Rivals of Aether style)
**Engine:** Phaser 3 + TypeScript
**Platform:** Web Browser (HTML5)
**Target:** Kids & Teens
**Price:** Free

## The Pitch

You are a fish. You've been pulled out of the water. You're flopping on dry land. And you're angry.

Armed with a toy fish and pure determination, slap, jump, and fight your way through rival fish, fishermen, and ultimately The Chef himself. A tale of rebellion, friendship, and freedom — told entirely through gameplay.

## Features

- **HP + Knockback combat** — As health drops, knockback increases. Low HP = one hit can launch you off the arena
- **5 playable fish** — Tuna, Carp, Squid, Pufferfish, Sakabambaspis (legendary)
- **10-fight arcade ladder** — From the open sea to the chef's kitchen
- **4 arenas** — Sea, Fish Market, Ship, Restaurant
- **Random events** — Lightning strikes, seagull grabs, poison rain
- **Weapon shop** — Earn coins, buy weapons and skins
- **Emotional story** — The Pufferfish companion arc will surprise you
- **Online multiplayer** (planned) — Fight friends via link or matchmaking
